About Us

The Fallen Mage is directed to gamers all around the world, with Arabic gamers in mind since it is going to fully support both English and Arabic languages. The game will provide a unique gaming experience. Allow the player to customize their character, then embark on a journey towards conquering the vast world of The Fallen Mage by meeting NPCs in different cities and doing favors and quests for them which in turn reward the player with currency to buy items from the in-game merchants, experience points that will increase the player’s level and many other rewards. Also they’ll have access to an arsenal of different combat abilities which will allow them to approach the battle exactly the way they want. There will also be a skill tree where the player can allocate points that they gain every level to customize their character. The player will also have an inventory where they can store all the items and equipment they collect. A great addition to the game will be the crafting system, where items that are collected during the game can be used for creating different equipment and items to make the character grow stronger. We’ll also introduce another way for increasing the character’s power, which is gear enhancement where you can use gems that are randomly dropped by monsters to enhance your equipment.
