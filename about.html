About Us

The Fallen Mage is a fantasy based open-world game made with the Unity game engine and it’s about a wizard who used to be the king of Pollentopes. He had control over the four natural elements (air, water, fire and earth), but one day a deceptive witch called Rosaria promised him unlimited power by doing a certain ritual, which then he did, but little did he know that it was a trick which robbed him of all his abilities. The ritual’s true purpose was to lock the wizard’s abilities in four different monsters scattered across the land of Pollentopes in order to gain control over his kingdom. The wizard then finds himself without any powers in a distant village, and he has to face many challenges to gain back his powers and overthrow the deceptive witch and restore his throne.
